Video to markdown!

Data Transmission Consent Required

To generate markdown snippets with video thumbnails, this extension needs to send the following data to our backend service:

  • Title and URL from the current webpage
  • Video Thumbnail URL for the image

Data transmission is only triggered when you click "Generate," sending the data to our backend service at api.video-to-markdown.marcomontalbano.com for necessary image generation and storage by Cloudinary.

No analytics, tracking, browsing history, personal information, or cookies are collected.

Without this consent, the extension cannot function. If you decline, the extension will be uninstalled.
