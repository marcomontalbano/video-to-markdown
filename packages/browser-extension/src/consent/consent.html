Video to markdown!

Simplify video embedding with our extension.

Extract thumbnail, add play icon, and generate markdown links instantly.

Welcome!

To generate markdown snippets with video thumbnails, this extension sends the page title, the URL, and the video thumbnail of the current webpage to api.video-to-markdown.marcomontalbano.com for image processing when you click the "Generate" button.
